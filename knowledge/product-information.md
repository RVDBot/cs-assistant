# Product Information

## Return Policy

- Customers who are dissatisfied with a purchase **cannot return the product**, regardless of how recently it was purchased.
  - *Note: This policy was applied to a customer who contacted support 10 days after purchase.*

> ⚠️ **Knowledge Base Note:** The agent's response was delivered in Dutch while the customer wrote in Spanish. This language mismatch has now been observed in multiple interactions — it is strongly recommended to ensure agents respond in the customer's language for better service quality.
